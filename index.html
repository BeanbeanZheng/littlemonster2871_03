Come see the little monster! The little unknown creature is a child belonging to the monster group: number 2871.

 

Next month is his rite of rites, and how exciting it is for other accomplices! As long as you pass the rite of passage, you can have your own name instead of numbering, and you can go to the wider human world and play without control.

 

But this is not the case for 2871, because he has a trait that normal monsters should not have - very timid.

 

The content of the rite of rites is to be tested by scaryness. If he can't pass him, he can only be a child forever. This will be a great shame for him and his family!

 

But it doesn't matter, there is still one month from the ceremony, and 30 days can make a huge change. So 2871 secretly decided: he would become the most scary monster in the town after 30 days.
